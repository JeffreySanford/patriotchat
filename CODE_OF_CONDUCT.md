# Contributor Covenant (Short)

Our project is dedicated to providing a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free experience for everyone.

Standards
- Be kind, patient, and constructive.
- Assume good intent and be open to feedback.
- Avoid abusive, harassing, or hateful language.

Reporting
- If you witness or experience behavior that violates this code of conduct, please contact the maintainers at `security@patriotchat.local` (or open an issue with the `security` label if email is not available).
- All reports will be handled in confidence and with respectful, timely follow-up.

Enforcement
- Repeated or egregious violations may result in removal from the community or other actions as deemed appropriate by the Governance Committee.
